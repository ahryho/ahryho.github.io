#### HELLO! GREAT TO SEE YOU AND THANK YOU FOR BEING HERE. LET ME INTRODUCE MYSELF.

**My name is Anastasiia.**

I am half Polish and half Ukrainian, but in many ways I feel that my home is the world. Over the years, I have lived in and moved between different countries, meeting people from diverse backgrounds, exploring new cultures, and trying to contribute positively wherever I am.

At heart, I am a dancer. I grew up practicing sport ballroom dance, which continues to be an important source of joy, balance, and energy for me. Dancing helps me reconnect with myself, stay present, and refill my inner reserves when life becomes challenging.

By training, I am a mathematician and data scientist, and professionally I have worked across academia, industry, and the public sector. My work has involved close collaboration with people from many disciplines, including biology, medicine, data science, and business management. I enjoy working in international and cross-cultural environments, learning from others, and being part of teams where different perspectives come together.

I am also a yoga practitioner and teacher. I have a long-standing daily Ashtanga Yoga practice, which I have maintained for over ten years. I have taught asanas (postures) and pranayama (breathing techniques) in various places across Europe and Asia. Yoga supports both my physical health and inner balance and has become an essential part of how I stay grounded and present.

I am driven by curiosity, a wish to understand the world more deeply, and a genuine desire to contribute to the well-being of others. I value meaningful work, community, and shared purpose, and I am especially drawn to environments that encourage reflection, kindness, and connection between people.
